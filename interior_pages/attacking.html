Basic Attacking

Attacking is essential if you want to be able to win games and sets against your opponents. Without knowing how to perform fundamental attacks, all you will be able to do is move which cannot be the only thing you do in a fighting game.

Attacking is broken up into two categories: Grounded Attacks and Aerial Attacks.

- To do a grounded attack press the A button.

- To do a jumping attack, pressing the X or Y button and then immediately press the A button

- Once you land after performing an aerial attack, you can perform another grounded attack to create a combo!
