Entering the Rivals of Aether Scene

So. You finally have the game installed; You played for a little while and found yourself a character you like;Now you ask yourself "Wow this game is kinda niche. Is there a community for it?"

YES! A large one that's continuously growing in fact!

This page will direct you to all the beginner and advanced Discord server dedicated to Rivals of Aether where you can meet people as enthusiastic about the game as you are!

*Note you will need a Discord account to participate in any of these servers*

The Great Rivals of Aether Discord Servers That You Should Enter

  • Rivals of Aether: The Academy Server

  • This is the perfect place to start out your Rivals of Aether career. Here you can study up on character's moves, ask for games, and sometimes get tips from the best of the best in the scene!

  • Rivals Recess

  • A smaller more dedicated server for allowing newer players to really get the ropes on the fundamentals without feeling overwhelmed.

  • Tournaments ran in Recess are broken down by skill levels so all players will constantly be playing against players of their caliber.

  • Rivals of Aether: The Official Discord

  • The main hub for the official game!

  • Here you'll find tournaments, updates, and sometimes you'll be lucky enough to run into developers who are willing to answer question!
