About Us:

My name is Neil Lakshmanan and I'm an avid fan and small-time competitor of platform fighting games.

Initially, I started off playing Super Smash Bros. Melee casually. However, like many others in the community, I soon discovered the competitive scene for the game and fell in love with the technicalities/flashiness of the game's engine

During the summer of 2020, one of my closets friend, who is also an avid fan of platform fighters as well, introduced me to Dan Forance's: "Rivals of Aether."

While I was skeptical at first, after hours of playing I soon came to realize that Rivals of Aether is a true love letter to the advanced platform fighters that I used to play and adore. Since the summer, I have competed in multiple Rivals of Aether tournaments, and have even managed to place 2nd in the NA Amateur Series.

With the knowledge I have developed over the months playing this wonderful game, I want to advocate for more players to give this brilliant game a try and now's better than ever.

Outside of Rivals, I am currently a junior and will be entering my fourth year at the New Jersey Institute of Technology pursuing an Information Technology Major.
